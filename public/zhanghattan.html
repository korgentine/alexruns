Zhanghattan is a 100-mile ultra marathon that takes place in New York City. It is a challenging race that tests the physical and mental strength of the participants. The race is named after Zhang Haotian, a Chinese ultra runner who is known for his endurance and speed. The course is a loop that takes runners through the streets of Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. The race is organized by the New York Road Runners and is known for its challenging terrain and unpredictable weather. The race is a popular event for ultra runners and is known for its supportive community and camaraderie. The race is a great way to test your limits and push yourself to new heights.
